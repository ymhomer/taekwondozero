About

Taekwon-Do is a Korean martial art, an effective art of self defence as well as a competitive sport. Taekwon-Do society was established in 2013 on TAR universities college.

Taekwondo society's aim is to provide an environment to self defence with physical, mental and spiritual training. Our society also help to bodybuilding, stamina, flexibility.
